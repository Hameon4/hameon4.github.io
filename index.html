Projects

SCOOTER INCIDENT REPORTING SYSTEM

This was my Senior Project where my team and I designed an attachable device to an e-scooter as well as the algorithm whose duty is to detect a fall, collision, or a crash from the escooter and transmit the data to the IoT platform via Wi-Fi. The IoT platform utilized was Thinger.io.

The algorithm we developed tracks the movement of the scooter. High spikes of frequency on the IoT platform's dashboard indicate a high possibility of an accident. The algorithm then sends a an email providing an alert as well as the device's ID and location, so help can arrive ASAP.

Much thought came among my team and I on improving the product such as adding a panic button that shall reduce the chances of false negatives. We also thought of icorporating a speaker/mic setup using VoIP to enable continuous communication with the monitoring team when required. And when possible, utilizing AI to teach the system how falls happen and make it predict them when one occurs.

On-Board Computer (OBC) Simulator

As my main task during my internship at the National Space Science and Technology Center (NSSTC) at UAEU, I developed a desktop (GUI) simulation software, using Python and Arduino, that acts as a replica to the telecommand/telemetry process in the satellite with UART as the communication protocol.

The first prototype was a CLI app, which I then modified it to a second and the go-to prototype: the GUI app.
